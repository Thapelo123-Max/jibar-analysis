## 📋 Project Overview

Comprehensive analysis of South Africa’s **3-month Johannesburg Interbank Average Rate (JIBAR)** from 2011 to 2026, its relationship with the Repo Rate, volatility during economic shocks, and implications of the transition to **ZARONIA**.

---

## 🚀 Key Findings

- Strong correlation (0.9834) between 3M JIBAR and Repo Rate
- Average 12-month rolling volatility: 0.347
- Significant spikes in volatility during 2020 (COVID-19) and 2022 (inflation shock)
